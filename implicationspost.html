The Effects of Quantum Computing on Various Industries

Posted by Kendall Thorne on January 19, 2023

Quantum computing is speculated as being a revolutionary new piece of technology that will have profound effects on many industries. There are many potential uses spanning across various fields like healthcare. Quantum computing would allow for quicker vaccine development as it would be able to simulate the creation and interactions of different molecules, a process that takes human scientists a long period of time. When it comes to transportation, quantum computing would allow delivery companies to find optimized routes accounting for various traffic patterns. It would also allow for better prediction of the weather. However, the industry that stands most to gain from this technology is the finance industry. Quantum computing would allow for accurate prediction of the stock market by calculating potential outcomes that were previously too unpredictable and numerous to fully understand. Despite all of these industries that stand to gain a lot from quantum computing, there are also many industries that would face major setbacks from this technology. The cybersecurity industry in particular is put at risk by this technology. Quantum computers would allow users to easily break encryptions and cryptographic algorithms because they have the ability to work with exponentially more factors than a classic computer. This puts the privacy and data security of technology users at substantial risk. In conclusion, while many industries stand to benefit from this new technology, quantum computing will have many negative implications in the cyber security industry.

Adapted from Timothy Hollebeek's Article, "The Impact of Quantum Computing on Society"
